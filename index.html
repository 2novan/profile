Gray, short hair slightly covers a long, gloomy face. Hollow green eyes, set lightly within their sockets, watch thoughtfully over the children they've loved for so long. A tattoo of a small eagle is subtly placed on the side of the left eye leaves a compelling memory of liberated love. This is the face of Gorlag Burningfury, a true mercenary among orcs. He stands tiny among others, despite his sturdy frame. There's something ambiguous about him, perhaps it's his warmth or perhaps it's simply a feeling of regret. But nonetheless, people tend to pretend to be his best friend, while secretly admiring him.
